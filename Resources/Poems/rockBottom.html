Rock Bottom

They say you only appreciate something
When you no longer have that very thing

Why did it take losing everything  about me
To appreciate who I am

What is it about reaching rock bottom
That all you can do is 
Go up

But like everything 
It's a state of mind
You can continue falling
Loosing yourself
But one day you need to decide
That
that exact moment is your 
Rock bottom.
